Privacy Policy

Last updated:

GameDice ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. This policy applies to our website and our mobile games published under the GameDice name.

Information we collect

  • Website analytics: We may collect non-identifying analytics such as page views, device, and approximate location. This helps us improve our site.
  • Game telemetry: Our games may collect anonymized gameplay events (e.g., level progress, crashes) to improve balance and stability.
  • Contact information: If you email us, we receive your email address and any information you include.

How we use information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our games and website
  • Fix bugs, prevent fraud, and ensure fair play
  • Provide support and respond to your messages

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers (e.g., analytics, crash reporting) who process it on our behalf under appropriate safeguards.

Children

Our products are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by local law. If you believe a child provided us information, contact us to request deletion.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined here, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your choices

  • You can opt out of analytics via your device settings where supported.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser. Some features may not function.
  • You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your information where applicable.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@gamedice.studio.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a new effective date.
